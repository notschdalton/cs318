Tomato Bisque

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 14 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 cup of half and half

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, saute garlic in butter for 1 minute. Then, stir in the flour until blended.
  2. Pour in the chicken broth, gradually. Stir in the tomatoes, paprika, garlic salt and basil until well blended.
  3. Bring the pot to a boil and cook for about 2 minutes - or until thickened.
  4. Using a blender, puree the soup until you reach your desired consistency. You will need to do this in batches.
  5. Add the pureed soup to a serving bowl and stir in the heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with cream and fresh basil.
